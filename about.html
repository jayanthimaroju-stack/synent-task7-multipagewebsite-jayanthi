About StudyHub 👋

We're just students like you — who wanted a better way to study and decided to build it ourselves.

Our Story 📖

How a study problem turned into a student solution

💡

It all started during exam season when two college students — Arjun and Priya — were frustrated with scattered notes, no study buddies nearby, and zero resources that actually made sense.

So they built StudyHub. A simple, friendly platform where students can find materials, track their goals, and not feel alone during tough study weeks.

Today, StudyHub helps students across 50+ colleges study better — and honestly, we're just getting started! 🚀
